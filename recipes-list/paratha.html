Aloo Paratha

Aloo paratha is a popular Indian flatbread stuffed with a spiced potato filling. Itâ€™s a delicious and hearty dish often enjoyed for breakfast or as a main meal. The potato filling is typically seasoned with various spices and herbs, making it flavorful and satisfying.

Recipe of Aloo Paratha

Ingredients:

  1. the Dough:
    • 2 cups whole wheat flour
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp oil or ghee (optional)
    • About 3/4 cup water
  2. For the Filling:
    • 2-3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
    • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
    • 1 tsp cumin seeds
    • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1/2 tsp garam masala
    • 2 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped
    • Salt to taste
  3. For Cooking:
    • Extra flour for dusting
    • Ghee or oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Dough:
    • Mix flour, salt, and oil (if using). Gradually add water to form a smooth dough. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Prepare Filling:
    • In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes with chopped green chilies, cumin seeds, coriander powder, turmeric, garam masala, cilantro, and salt.
  3. Assemble Parathas:
    • Divide dough into balls and roll each into a small circle.
    • Place a portion of the potato filling in the center, fold the edges over to seal, and gently roll out again into a larger circle.
  4. Cook:
    • Heat a griddle or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook each paratha for about 30 seconds on one side, flip, and cook for another 30 seconds. Brush with ghee or oil, flip again, and cook until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve:
    • Enjoy hot with yogurt, pickle, or curry.

Aloo paratha is loved for its rich, savory filling and can be customized with various spices and herbs according to taste.
